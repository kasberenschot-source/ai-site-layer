How it works

  
Three steps to AI-ready

  

    

      
1

      
We build your MCP server

      
We analyze your website and build a server that exposes your content — prices, availability, menus, events — as structured tools.

    

    

      
2

      
Add one meta-tag

      
Drop a single line into your site's HTML. Any AI agent with a discovery tool will find your server automatically.

      
<meta name="mcp-server"
  content="https://your-server/mcp">

    

    

      
3

      
AI agents find you

      
When someone asks an AI about your business, it queries your server directly — getting accurate, real-time answers instead of outdated scraped content.
